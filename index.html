Places Must Visit

Gaddafi Stadium

I visited it in April 2022

Gaddafi Stadium, previously known as Lahore Stadium is a cricket stadium in Lahore, Pakistan and the home ground of Lahore Qalandars. It is owned by the Pakistan Cricket Board. With a capacity of 27,000, it is the fourth largest cricket stadium of Pakistan.

Azhar Ali Academy

I visited it in April 2022 and December 2021

Former Test captain Azhar Ali has announced that he is launching a cricket academy in Valencia town, Lahore from December 2020. The right-hander, Azhar has represented the Green Caps in 134 fixtures with 7974 runs to his name, including 20 centuries and 43 half-centuries. The academy is well equipped with qualified coaches, physiotherapist and video analyst. Star cricketers will visit too in coming days.
